WHO WE ARE

Kalpvraksh or Kalpavriksha (कल्पवृक्ष), also known as kalpataru, kalpadruma or kalpapādapa, is a wish-fulfilling divine tree in Hindu mythology. It is mentioned in Sanskrit literature from the earliest sources. Kalpavrikshas are wish-granting trees which fulfill the desires of people in initial stages of worldly cycle Kalpvraksh are the team of social worker’s to create lasting change in the lives of children, families and communities living in poverty and injustice.
